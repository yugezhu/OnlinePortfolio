PLUKKA CHATER HOUSE

FINE JEWELERY RETAIL STORE IN CENTRAL, HK

Client

Plukka

LOCATION

Chater House, Central,
Hong Kong

STATUS

Completed 2016

DESIGN FIRM

plusclover

DESIGN TEAM

Clover Lee, Yuge Zhu,
Alyssa Tang, Helina Lass,
Carman Liu

The boutique is enclosed by a mysterious, expansive curved wall that gently draws you into an intimate space. Upon entering, customers are ensconced by the soft, plush materials of the upholstery and carpeting. The custom-designed wallpaper featuring drawings of flowing hair by the Chinese contemporary ink artist Hong Chun Zhang adds to the sensuality of the space. Though counter-intuitive for a small space, the curved exterior wall pressurizes the boutique interior to increase the intimacy for customers, creating a private jewelry viewing room, while a horizontal band of mirrors amplifies the display inside.
