CRACK IT

ARCHITECTURAL RESEARCH LAB IN KENNEDY TOWN, HK

LOCATION

Kennedy Town,
Hong Kong

DESIGN TIME

2013. 6 (academic year 2)

TUTOR

Christiane Lange, HKU

PROJECT TYPE

individual

The scheme is an architectural research centre with the total area of 2500m2. The strategy is to blur the rigid boundaries from water to the land by topographic architecture. The building is composed of four parts: ‘the cliff’, ‘the bay’, ‘the pier’ and ‘the island’. ‘The island’ as the research lab float as the sea water fluctuate, providing a real-time experience between architecture and nature.
