At the legendary Burlington Arcade in Piccadilly, London, the design of the Plukka London takes advantage of the store’s intimate scale to envelope the visitor in space that is elegant and luxurious, yet experimental and edgy. By pushing the undulating landscape of showcases along the periphery and against Plukka’s signature pin-striped wallpaper, the dark and light-absorbent material palette accentuates the brilliance of the jewelry.
